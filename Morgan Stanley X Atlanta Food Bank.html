Hackathon Project: Food Foresight

Team Members: Vaibhav Goyal, Sepehr Eshaghian, Elizabeth King, Gowtham Thatavarthi, Manas Mishra

Mentors: Burnard Pull, Senjuti Kundu

Challenge

  • Equitable Food Distribution
  • Consistent Delivery to Food Partners
  • Culturally-Centric and Quality Food For Pantries
  • Minimum Delivery Barrier

Proposed Solution: Forecasting & Statistical Model

Our solution involves predicting order amounts based on the pantry's order history, analyzing and identifying cultural relevance for each pantry, classifying the nutritional value of delivered consumables, and clustering pantries to overcome the minimum delivery barrier.
